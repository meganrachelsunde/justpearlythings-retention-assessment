JustPearlyThings Tools

Two browser-based tools prepared for Pearl Davis and the JustPearlyThings team: one for audience retention analytics, one for clip and statement risk review.

Analytics

Audience Retention Assessment

Score videos and clips using Studio metrics, public proxy data, and the Retention Instrument structure rubric.

  • CTR, APV, AVD, intro retention, spike depth
  • Views, likes, comments, 48-hour velocity
  • PPPS score, diagnosis, and next edit
Open retention tool Risk

Clip & Statement Risk Assessor

Review clip language, context, and statement exposure before publishing or pitching a cut.

  • Risk factors and severity tiers
  • Statement-level review workflow
  • Publishing caution and mitigation prompts
Open risk tool

These tools run in the browser. Any entered Studio data stays local to the browser unless exported by the user.
