A pre-publish triage instrument for JustPearlyThings. It surfaces where a cut — or a thing said on camera — carries legal or reputational exposure, before it ships. Two modes, one discipline: see it coming.

This is a triage aid, not legal advice. It structures judgment and flags exposure for a human to weigh — it does not determine liability, and it isn't a substitute for an attorney. For anything that scores into Hold or Escalate, route it to qualified counsel before publishing.
